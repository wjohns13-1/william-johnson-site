What I've Done

Experience to Date

2021 – Present
Tennis Coaching Career

Started coaching young players during high school summers, then returned from an LDS mission in 2024 to coach a high school varsity team. Has since worked at Last Chance Tennis (Helena, MT), Sunpro Tennis & Pickleball (Springville, UT), and Ivory Ridge (Lehi, UT).

Summer 2024
USPTA Certification

Earned USPTA certification, validating professional coaching standards. Currently working toward a Level 3 certification — the highest designation in the USPTA system.

Summer 2025
Mouratoglou Academy — France

Spent a month living and training at the prestigious Mouratoglou Tennis Academy in France — one of the world's premier coaching institutions. Immersed in elite-level methodology and culture daily.

2025 – Present
Cedar J Tennis — Founder

Founded my own coaching and racket restringing business upon returning from France. Offered private and group lessons in Helena, MT. Still actively running the racket restringing arm of the business.

Ongoing
Coaching All Ages

Has coached players ranging from 3–4 year olds in their first introduction to the sport, all the way to adults 80 years and older. Skilled at adapting instruction style to any age, level, or learning style.

In Progress
Buttermilk Syrup Venture

Collaborating with his sister to develop and bring a buttermilk syrup product to market — an early example of entrepreneurial initiative outside the tennis world.

What I Bring

Anticipated Contributions

I came into tennis later than most — picking it up seriously in high school — which gives me a unique empathy for players who are starting from scratch. That perspective drives everything I want to build.

Tennis for Underserved Communities

Tennis is often seen as a sport for the privileged. I want to change that — building accessible programs, affordable coaching models, and community-first academies that open the sport to people who've never had a chance to pick up a racket.

AI-Enhanced Coaching

I've become well-versed in using AI for scheduling, lesson planning, and player development. I believe coaching businesses that embrace technology will deliver better outcomes — and I intend to lead that charge.

Creative Ideation & Entrepreneurship

I have a strong imagination and genuinely love creating new things. That creativity, paired with a business education from BYU, means I'll consistently bring fresh ideas — whether to the court, to a startup, or to a community.

World-Class Academy Model

Inspired by my time at Mouratoglou, I hope to one day build a tennis academy that rivals the best in the world — one that emphasizes character development alongside athletic excellence.
