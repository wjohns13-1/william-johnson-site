About Me

Coach. Entrepreneur. Explorer.

I'm a sophomore at Brigham Young University studying Entrepreneurship, and a USPTA certified tennis coach working toward my Level 3 certification. I've been coaching since 2021 — from 3-year-olds picking up a racket for the first time, to 80-year-olds who've played their whole lives.


In the summer of 2025 I spent a month living and training at the Mouratoglou Tennis Academy in France — one of the premier coaching institutions in the world. That experience inspired me to found Cedar J Tennis, my own coaching and racket restringing business based in Helena, MT.


Outside of tennis I'm passionate about travel — I've visited 47 of the 50 U.S. states and 12 countries across Europe and Central America, and I'm fluent in Spanish. I believe that seeing the world is one of the best ways to grow as a person and a leader.
